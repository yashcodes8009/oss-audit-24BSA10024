Open Source Manifesto
Date: 30 March 2026
----------------------------------------

On 30 March 2026, I reflect on the importance of open source.
I use git regularly, and it represents the spirit of collaboration.
To me, freedom means It helps me manage my code and track changes easily — the ability to learn, modify, and share.
I believe in building Freedom to use, modify and share software and making it accessible to everyone.
Open source is not just code, it is a philosophy of transparency and community.
